Meet the students

Hello! My name is Răzvan Cîrlugea and I am a Web Developer/Instructor. During the course I teach at IT School, I taught my students how to use Git & GitHub. As practice, I challenged them to fork this project and complete their contact information.

Take a look at their LinkedIn, GitHub, CodePen și StackBlitz profiles, and if you like what you see, don't hesitate to contact them for a job!
